Academic Position Ref. No.: W123-2023

Professor (W2) of Computer Science

Department of Electrical Engineering and Information Technology
Aachen
Posted: 15.06.2023
Application deadline: 15.07.2023
Full-time

Job Description

The Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Information Technology at Vikram Sarabhai Institute of Sciences invites applications for a professorship (W2) in Computer Science with a focus on Software Engineering to commence at the earliest possible date.

Responsibilities include:

  • Teaching in bachelor's and master's degree programs in German and English
  • Research in the field of software engineering
  • Supervision of student projects and theses
  • Participation in academic self-administration

Requirements

Essential Qualifications:

  • Completed university degree in computer science or related field
  • Outstanding PhD in computer science or related discipline
  • At least five years of professional experience, including three years outside academia
  • Excellent teaching skills and pedagogical aptitude

Desirable Qualifications:

  • Experience in acquiring and managing research projects
  • International research experience
  • Knowledge of German (or willingness to learn within two years)

Application Process

Vikram Sarabhai Institute of Sciences is committed to increasing the percentage of female faculty members and therefore particularly encourages applications from women.

Applications from severely disabled applicants will be given preferential consideration in case of equal qualification.

How to Apply:

Please submit your application with the usual documents (CV, certificates, list of publications, teaching evaluations if available) by 15.07.2023 to:

Vikram Sarabhai Institute of Sciences
Apuja
752103 Odisha
India
Or by email: bewerbungen@vsis.in
